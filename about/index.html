Built for Alberta's Climate. Built to Last.

Alberta winters are hard on buildings. Freeze-thaw cycles, moisture infiltration, and dramatic temperature swings break down exterior wall systems faster here than almost anywhere else in the country. AxisLayer Exteriors was built around one idea: do the work right the first time, so it holds up for the long term.

We specialize exclusively in exterior wall systems — stucco, EIFS, parging, and culture stone — for homeowners, builders, and property managers across Edmonton and the surrounding region. No subs, no shortcuts. Just experienced hands on every project.

  • Climate-tested installation methods We follow Alberta-specific detailing for drainage, air sealing, and thermal performance — not generic national specs.
  • Residential & commercial exterior work From single-family homes to multi-unit buildings — stucco, EIFS, parging, and culture stone.
  • Transparent quoting, no surprises We assess the scope before we price it — so the number we give you is the number you pay.
