Reliable Exterior Wall Systems for Alberta Homes

AxisLayer Exteriors provides stucco, EIFS, and parging services across Edmonton and surrounding areas. We focus on durable exterior systems designed for Alberta’s freeze-thaw climate, moisture exposure, and long-term building protection.

Our work is centered on proper installation, repair, and maintenance of exterior wall systems that improve insulation, prevent water damage, and extend the life of residential and commercial buildings.

  • Stucco installation and repair (traditional and acrylic systems)
  • EIFS insulation and exterior finishing systems
  • Foundation parging and waterproof exterior coatings

We work with homeowners, builders, and property managers who need reliable exterior finishing that performs in Alberta weather conditions.
