Fireplace & Interior Feature Wall Upgrades

Stacked stone feature walls are one of the highest-impact interior upgrades for Edmonton homes. They are most commonly used for fireplaces, TV walls, and living room feature installations, delivering a modern architectural finish without full renovation work.

🔥 Fireplace Feature Walls (Primary Use Case)

Fireplace installations are the most popular application for stacked stone. The linear stone pattern creates a modern focal point that upgrades the entire living space instantly.

Where Stacked Stone Works Best

  • Fireplace Feature Walls: Primary high-impact interior upgrade.
  • TV Wall Builds: Clean architectural backdrop for mounted screens.
  • Living Room Accent Walls: Adds depth, contrast, and texture.

Why Homeowners Choose Stacked Stone

Stacked stone delivers a premium architectural finish with significantly faster installation compared to traditional masonry. It is ideal for homeowners looking for a modern upgrade without full-scale renovation disruption.

Installation Process

  1. Preparation: Wall inspection and proper backing installation.
  2. Layout Design: Planning linear stone alignment for clean visual flow.
  3. Installation: High-bond adhesive application and precise stone placement.
  4. Finishing: Edge detailing and final alignment for seamless appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
Most stacked stone feature walls are completed in 1–2 days depending on size and preparation.

Can stacked stone be installed on existing drywall?
Yes, but proper backing and surface preparation are required for long-term durability.

Is stacked stone good for fireplaces?
Yes. It is one of the most commonly used fireplace finishes due to its modern look and heat resistance.

Does stacked stone work for TV walls?
Yes. It is widely used as a clean, modern backdrop for mounted televisions and media walls.

What areas do you service?
Edmonton, St. Albert, Sherwood Park, and surrounding areas.
