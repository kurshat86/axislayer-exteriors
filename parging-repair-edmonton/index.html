Fast & Reliable Parging Repair Services

Don't let a small crack turn into a major foundation issue. We specialize in parging repair in Edmonton, helping homeowners restore their curb appeal and protect their concrete from Alberta’s harsh weather. Whether it's a minor patch or a full exterior refresh, we get the job done right.

We Fix Damaged Parging

Parging is your foundation’s first line of defense. When it begins to fail, it’s not just an eyesore—it’s an invitation for moisture to settle against your concrete. Our repair services focus on identifying the cause of the failure and providing a seamless fix that blends with your existing exterior. If you aren't sure if the damage is purely cosmetic or needs urgent attention, our parging decision guide can help you qualify the severity of your foundation's condition.

Small Repairs vs. Full Redo

Not every foundation needs a total overhaul. We assess the damage to determine the most cost-effective path for you. To help you plan your budget, you can view the typical pricing for parging work across the Edmonton area, ranging from small patches to full resurfacing.

  • Minor Patching: Ideal for hairline cracks or small localized chips where the rest of the parging is still well-bonded.
  • Sectional Repair: Used when specific areas (like corners or near garden beds) have deteriorated due to moisture.
  • Full Resurfacing: If the original parging is hollow, peeling, or crumbling across the entire wall, a full redo ensures long-term protection.

Common Issues We Solve

Our team handles all types of parging failure common in the Edmonton area, including:

  • Cracked Parging: Filling and sealing cracks caused by settling or freeze-thaw cycles.
  • Spalling & Crumbling: Removing "soft" or salt-damaged cement and applying a high-strength coating.
  • Hollow Spots: Detecting areas where the parging is peeling off the foundation but hasn't fallen away completely yet.

Parging Repair FAQ

Can I repair parging in the winter?
Parging requires specific temperatures to cure correctly. While we typically work during the warmer months, contact us to discuss the current weather conditions.

How long does a parging repair last?
When properly bonded and applied to a clean surface, a professional repair can last for many years, depending on the condition of the underlying concrete.

Will the repair match my old parging?
We do our best to match the texture and color, though new cement often appears slightly different than aged material until it fully cures and weathers.
