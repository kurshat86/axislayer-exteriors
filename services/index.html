Not Sure Which Service You Need?

The most common question we get is how these services relate to each other. The short version: stucco and EIFS are above-grade exterior wall finishes — stucco is simpler and lower cost, EIFS adds insulation and is better suited to energy efficiency upgrades. Parging is for foundation walls at and below grade — a different material and application to the above-grade wall systems. Culture stone is an accent finish — used on fireplaces, feature walls, and select exterior sections rather than as a full cladding system.

If you're still not sure which applies to your project, send us a few photos through our contact page and we'll point you in the right direction.
