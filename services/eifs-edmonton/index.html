EIFS Installation in Edmonton

EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems) used for improving exterior insulation, reducing heat loss, and protecting building envelopes in Alberta’s freeze-thaw climate.

Overview

EIFS is a multi-layer exterior wall system made of insulation boards, reinforcing mesh, base coat, and finish coat. It improves thermal performance and protects exterior walls from moisture and temperature stress.

When this service is needed

  • Energy efficiency upgrades for older buildings
  • New residential or commercial construction
  • Exterior wall renovation or resurfacing
  • Moisture or insulation performance issues

Process

  1. Surface inspection and wall assessment
  2. Installation of insulation boards
  3. Application of base coat and fiberglass mesh
  4. Finish coat application and curing

Why it matters in Edmonton

Edmonton’s long winters and freeze-thaw cycles put stress on exterior walls. EIFS reduces heat loss, improves energy efficiency, and helps protect the structure from moisture penetration and exterior cracking.

EIFS is commonly used in Edmonton for exterior insulation upgrades, energy-efficient wall systems, and modern commercial and residential building envelopes.
