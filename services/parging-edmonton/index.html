Fix Cracked, Flaking & Damaged Foundation Walls

Is your foundation starting to crack, peel, or look worn out? Parging restores the surface, improves curb appeal, and helps protect your foundation from moisture damage in Edmonton’s harsh freeze-thaw climate.

What is Foundation Parging?

Parging is a thin cement-based coating applied to the exterior of foundation walls. It covers exposed concrete, seals minor surface damage, and creates a clean, uniform finish.

Signs Your Foundation Needs Parging

  • Cracked or crumbling surface
  • Parging peeling or falling off
  • Exposed or unfinished concrete foundation
  • Moisture stains or discoloration
  • Rough, worn, or aging exterior walls

What Happens If You Don’t Fix It

  • Moisture can penetrate and weaken the surface
  • Cracks expand due to freeze-thaw cycles
  • Concrete deterioration accelerates over time
  • Foundation appearance continues to worsen

Common Foundation Parging Problems in Edmonton

Most homeowners notice issues only after visible damage appears. Below are the most common parging problems we see in Edmonton:

  • Cracked foundation parging
  • Parging peeling or falling off
  • Moisture-damaged foundation walls
  • Exposed or unfinished concrete foundations

Our Parging Process

  1. Inspection of the foundation condition
  2. Surface cleaning and preparation
  3. Repair of cracks and damaged areas
  4. Application of bonding layer (if needed)
  5. Application of parging coat and finish

Why Parging is Important in Edmonton

Edmonton’s climate causes repeated freeze-thaw cycles that put stress on exposed concrete. Without protection, foundations can crack, absorb moisture, and deteriorate faster.
