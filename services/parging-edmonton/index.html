Foundation Parging in Edmonton

Foundation parging and exterior concrete coating for protecting exposed foundations, improving curb appeal, and preventing moisture damage in Edmonton’s freeze-thaw climate.

Overview

Parging is a thin mortar or cement coating applied to exposed foundation walls. It protects concrete surfaces from moisture penetration, surface deterioration, and improves the appearance of aging foundations.

When parging is needed

  • Cracked or crumbling foundation surface
  • Exposed or unfinished concrete walls
  • Moisture staining or water seepage signs
  • Exterior foundation cosmetic upgrade

Process

  1. Inspection of foundation condition
  2. Surface cleaning and crack preparation
  3. Application of bonding layer (if required)
  4. Application of parging coat and finishing texture

Why parging matters in Edmonton

Edmonton’s freeze-thaw cycles and soil moisture levels put constant stress on foundation walls. Without protection, exposed concrete can crack, absorb moisture, and deteriorate over time. Parging helps extend foundation life and reduce long-term repair costs.
