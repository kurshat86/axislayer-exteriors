Stucco Installation & Repair in Edmonton

Stucco installation, repair, and resurfacing for residential and commercial buildings in Edmonton. Designed to withstand Alberta’s freeze-thaw cycles, moisture exposure, and exterior cracking.

Overview

Stucco is a cement-based exterior finish applied over wall systems to create a durable, weather-resistant surface. In Edmonton, it is commonly used for both new construction and repair of cracked or aging exterior walls.

When stucco service is needed

  • Cracks in exterior walls or surface delamination
  • Water intrusion or moisture staining
  • Damaged or aging stucco finishes
  • New home or building exterior finishing

Process

  1. Site inspection and damage assessment
  2. Removal or preparation of existing surface
  3. Application of base coat and reinforcement mesh (if required)
  4. Final stucco coat and texture finishing

Why stucco matters in Edmonton

Edmonton’s freeze-thaw cycles cause expansion and contraction in exterior materials. Stucco systems must be properly installed to prevent cracking, moisture intrusion, and long-term structural damage. Proper installation directly affects durability and lifespan.
