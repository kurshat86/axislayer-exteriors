2026 Culture Stone Pricing Guide for Edmonton Homeowners

Planning a stone veneer project and want a realistic cost before getting quotes? This guide breaks down culture stone installation pricing in Edmonton for 2026, including material types, installation conditions, and key cost drivers.

What Does Culture Stone Cost Per Square Foot in Edmonton?

Installed culture stone in Edmonton typically ranges from $25 to $55 per square foot. Material costs usually account for $10–$22 per square foot depending on stone type and quality.

In most residential projects, total installed pricing typically falls between $30 and $50 per square foot for standard feature walls.

Stacked Stone vs Ledgestone Pricing

  • Ledgestone panels: Faster installation due to pre-assembled format. Typical installed cost: $25–$38 per sq ft.
  • Stacked stone: Individually set pieces requiring more labour and precision. Typical installed cost: $35–$55 per sq ft.
  • River rock: More irregular layout and detailing. Typical installed cost: $38–$55 per sq ft.

Interior vs Exterior Installation Cost

  • Interior installation: No exterior weatherproofing required. Typically 15–25% lower cost.
  • Exterior installation: Requires moisture barriers, flashing, and freeze-thaw protection systems. Higher labour and material requirements.

Repair Pricing

  • Minor repair: $300–$600
  • Section repair: $1,000–$3,000+
  • Full replacement: Comparable to new installation plus demolition

What Affects Culture Stone Cost?

  • Total square footage
  • Stone type and profile
  • Wall height and access difficulty
  • Substrate condition and prep work required
  • Corner pieces and trim complexity
  • Interior vs exterior scope

Example Project Pricing

  • Fireplace (40–60 sq ft): $1,200–$2,400
  • Feature wall (80–120 sq ft): $2,000–$4,500
  • Exterior columns (20–40 sq ft): $900–$2,000
  • Facade section (150–250 sq ft): $5,000–$12,000+
