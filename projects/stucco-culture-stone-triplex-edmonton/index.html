Modern Residential Triplex Exterior with Multi-Tone Stucco and Culture Stone in Edmonton

This project features a contemporary two-story triplex in Edmonton, finished with high-contrast acrylic stucco and a combination of premium exterior materials. The design blends deep charcoal black and light beige stucco with charcoal culture stone and wood-textured siding for a bold, modern appearance.

The system is built using an advanced EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System), incorporating EPS insulation, reinforced fiberglass mesh, and a durable basecoat. This creates an energy-efficient and weather-resistant building envelope suited for Alberta’s climate.

Architectural detailing is achieved through integrated aesthetic reveal joints. These horizontal and vertical grooves form clean geometric patterns across the façade, enhancing depth and visual structure while maintaining a sleek modern finish.

The exterior is further elevated with charcoal stack stone accents and wood-textured siding, introducing natural textures that contrast with the smooth acrylic stucco. The foundation is finished with protective parging, and the rooftop includes a glass railing system that reinforces the modern residential design.

  • System Type: EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System)
  • Insulation: EPS Foam
  • Reinforcement: Fiberglass Mesh + Basecoat
  • Finish: Acrylic Stucco (Black & Beige)
  • Accents: Charcoal Culture Stone + Wood-Textured Siding
  • Details: Horizontal & Vertical Reveal Joints
  • Additional: Foundation Parging + Glass Railings
  • Project Type: Residential Triplex
