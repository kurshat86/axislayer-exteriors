Modern Residential EIFS and Acrylic Stucco Duplex in Edmonton

This project features a contemporary two-story duplex in Edmonton finished with a multi-tone EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System). The design combines clean architectural lines with high-contrast acrylic stucco finishes to create a modern, durable exterior.

The system includes EPS insulation, reinforced fiberglass mesh, a durable basecoat, and a high-performance acrylic finish. The primary wall surfaces use a light beige/grey tone, contrasted by dark grey accents along the building edges and central separation to define each unit.

Architectural detail is achieved through integrated reveal joints. Horizontal grooves are placed between floor levels and above upper windows, while vertical grooves beside window frames create a structured panel effect that enhances the overall façade.

The side elevation incorporates a material transition, with brick veneer on the lower level and dark acrylic stucco above. The foundation is finished with protective parging, ensuring a clean appearance and long-term durability in Edmonton’s climate.

  • System Type: EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System)
  • Insulation: EPS Foam
  • Reinforcement: Fiberglass Mesh + Basecoat
  • Finish: Acrylic Stucco (Multi-Tone)
  • Details: Horizontal & Vertical Reveal Joints
  • Additional: Brick Veneer + Foundation Parging
  • Project Type: Residential Duplex
