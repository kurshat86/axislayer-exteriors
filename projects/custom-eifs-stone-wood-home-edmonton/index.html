Modern Custom EIFS and Multi-Material Cladding Home in Edmonton

This high-end two-story custom home in Edmonton showcases a premium exterior built with a combination of EIFS stucco, natural stone, and wood-textured siding. The design focuses on clean modern lines while maintaining strong material contrast for architectural depth and curb appeal.

The main exterior system is an Exterior Insulation and Finish System (EIFS), consisting of continuous foam insulation, reinforced fiberglass mesh, a cementitious basecoat, and a durable light beige acrylic finish. This system significantly improves thermal performance and reduces energy loss, which is critical in Edmonton’s climate.

Dark stacked culture stone is used at the main entrance and structural columns to create a strong visual base. This is paired with wood-textured siding on select architectural sections, including garage and bump-out features, adding warmth and texture to the design.

The long side elevation demonstrates the strength of EIFS as a seamless exterior system, covering large surface areas without visible joints. The result is a weather-tight, highly insulated home that balances durability with a refined modern aesthetic.

  • System Type: EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System)
  • Insulation: Continuous EPS Foam Board
  • Reinforcement: Fiberglass Mesh + Basecoat
  • Finish: Light Beige Acrylic Stucco
  • Accent Materials: Dark Culture Stone + Wood Textured Siding
  • Project Type: Custom Residential Home
